## Mataki na 1: Fahimtar Intanet da Browser

### Intanet

Gizo-gizo ce da ke haɗa na’urori da juna ta hanyar sadarwa (network). A takaice, Intanet hanya ce da mutane da kwamfutoci ke amfani da ita don aika da karɓar bayanai.

### Browser

Shirin kwamfuta ne kamar Chrome, Firefox, ko Safari, wanda ke ba ka damar duba shafukan yanar gizo.

> **Aiki**: Gwada buɗe Chrome ko Firefox, rubuta `www.google.com`, ka lura yadda browser ke aiki.

---

## Mataki na 2: Fahimtar Yanar Gizo (Website)

### Website

Taron bayanai ne da aka shirya a cikin shafuka (pages), ana iya ziyartarsu ta hanyar Intanet.

### Webpage

Kamar shafi ɗaya a cikin littafi. Kowanne shafi yana da adireshi (URL).

> **Aiki**: Ziyarci websites kamar [Wikipedia](https://www.wikipedia.org) ko [BBC Hausa](https://www.bbc.com/hausa). Ka lura da yadda suke da shafuka daban-daban.

---

## Mataki na 3: Fahimtar HTML - "Tattara Ginin Gida"

**HTML** (HyperText Markup Language) shine tsarin abin da ake gani a website — kamar rubutu, hotuna, links, da headings.

**Misali na HTML**:

```
<h1>Sannu Duniya</h1>
<p>Ina murna da koyon web development!</p>
```


> **Aiki**: Rubuta wannan code a cikin Notepad, ajiye shi da `.html` (misali, `index.html`), sannan ka buɗe a browser ka ga abin da ya fito.

> **YouTube Tutorial** [HTML](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PL6Ro1qG9QKucphy-IsQ_9z7tSo1ZPd9zZ&si=qILpdC1kINobU_AG)

## Mataki na 4: Fahimtar CSS - "Yin Kwalliya da Tsarawa"

**CSS** (Cascading Style Sheets) ana amfani da shi don kyautata kallo da ado na shafi — kamar canza launi, girman rubutu, ko shimfiɗa abubuwa.

**Misali na CSS**:

```
h1 {
  color: blue;
  text-align: center;
}
```

> **Aiki**: Haɗa CSS da HTML ɗinka don canza launin rubutu ko shimfiɗa abubuwa.

---

## Mataki na 5: Fahimtar JavaScript - "Ruhin Gida"

**JavaScript** yana kawo rai da motsi a shafinka — kamar buttons da ake danna, forms da ake cikawa, ko abubuwa da ke canzawa kai tsaye.

**Misali na JavaScript**:

```
<button onclick="alert('Sannu, Abokina!')">Danna Ni</button>
```

> **Aiki**: Saka wannan button a shafinka, ka danna ka ga sakon da ya bayyana.

## Mataki na 6: Tattara Abubuwa

Haɗa waɗannan abubuwa:

- **HTML** don tsari.
- **CSS** don ado.
- **JavaScript** don motsi.

Wannan ake kira **Front-end Development** — ɓangaren da mutane ke gani da amfani da shi.

## Mataki na 9: Fahimtar Back-End

Bayan ka iya Front-end, za ka iya koyon:

- **Servers**: Yadda ake sarrafa bayanai a bayan gida.
- **Databases**: Adana bayanai kamar user info.
- **Backend Languages**: Kamar [Python (Django, Flask)](https://www.djangoproject.com) ko [Node.js](https://nodejs.org).

---

## Mataki na 10: Tafiya Cikin Ƙwarewa

Ƙara koyo akan:

- **Responsive Design**: Yadda shafi zai dace da waya da kwamfuta.
- **APIs**: Yadda websites ke magana da juna.
- **Deployment**: Sanya website a duniya ta gani.
- **Security Basics**: Kare bayanai da amfanin users.
